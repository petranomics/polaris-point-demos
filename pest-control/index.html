What We Do

Our Pest Control Services

Comprehensive protection for your home and business against Central Texas pests.

Termite Control

Complete termite inspections, liquid treatments, bait stations, and pre-construction protection to safeguard your biggest investment.

Ant Extermination

Targeted treatments for fire ants, carpenter ants, and sugar ants — inside and out. We eliminate colonies at the source.

Roach Control

Advanced gel baits, growth regulators, and perimeter treatments to eliminate roach infestations and prevent recurrence.

Mosquito Control

Monthly barrier sprays, larvicide treatments, and In2Care trap systems to reclaim your outdoor spaces.

Rodent Control

Humane trapping, exclusion sealing, and bait stations for rats and mice. We find entry points and seal them for good.

Spider Removal

Treatment for brown recluse, black widows, and common house spiders. Interior and exterior web removal included.

Wasp & Bee Removal

Safe nest removal for wasps, yellow jackets, and hornets. Bee-friendly relocation services available for honeybees.

Scorpion Control

Perimeter barrier treatments and harborage elimination to keep scorpions out of your home — a common Texas concern.

Bed Bug Treatment

Heat treatments and targeted chemical applications to eliminate bed bugs at every life stage. Discreet, thorough service.

About Us

Your Neighbors in Pest Control

Pete's Termite & Pest Control was founded in 2005 by Pete Hernandez, a lifelong Austin resident and Board Certified Entomologist with a passion for protecting families and homes from unwanted pests.

What started as a one-truck operation has grown into one of Central Texas's most trusted pest control companies — but we've never lost the personal touch that built our reputation. Every technician is background-checked, state-licensed, and treats your home like it's their own.

We believe in Integrated Pest Management (IPM): solutions that are effective, environmentally responsible, and safe for your family and pets. We don't just spray and pray — we find the root cause and fix it.
