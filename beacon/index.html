What Beacon Does

One System. Endless Marketing.

Beacon is seeded with everything about your business — your services, brand voice, competitors, and customers. Then it works for you, every day.

Always-On Chat

Ask anything about your marketing — Beacon already knows your business and responds with context-aware answers in seconds.

Auto-Scheduled Content

Weekly social posts. Monthly newsletters. Blog drafts on a calendar. All written in your brand voice — ready for you to approve and publish.

Competitor Watch

Beacon checks your competitors' websites and reviews daily. Get summaries of what they're doing — and what gaps you can exploit.
