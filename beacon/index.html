The problem

You know you should be marketing online. You don't have time.

Most small businesses know consistent marketing is what separates them from the competition. None of them have hours each week to write social posts, draft newsletters, or respond thoughtfully to every review.

The shortcuts haven't worked either. ChatGPT doesn't know your business — every prompt starts from zero. Freelancers cost $500–$1,500/month for recycled templates. Hiring a marketer runs $4,000+/month and takes three months to get up to speed.

Beacon is the missing middle: a system that learns your business once, then works for you every day.

How it works

Three steps. About a week to set up. Then it just runs.

  1. We seed it with your business.

    Services, brand voice, competitors, customer reviews, market data — all loaded during onboarding. By day two, Beacon writes like someone who's worked at your company for years.

  2. It works on a schedule.

    Weekly social posts. Monthly newsletters. Blog drafts. Competitor reports. Review response drafts. All generated automatically and delivered to your dashboard.

  3. You approve in 10 minutes.

    Review the drafts, tweak if needed, hit post. You can also chat with Beacon anytime — ask for an email, a strategy idea, a response to a tough customer.
