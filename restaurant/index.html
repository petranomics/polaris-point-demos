Our Story

Started in a Backyard. Built for Austin.

Smoke & Stone began with weekend cookouts, one handmade pit, and a promise to never rush the process. What started as neighbors lining up for brisket became a full-time craft rooted in patience, smoke, and serious attention to detail.

Today, our pitmasters still trim every cut by hand, season with house rubs, and smoke low and slow until it is exactly right. We serve East Austin with pride and keep the same backyard spirit in every plate we hand over the counter.
