Our Commitment

{{businessName}} is committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We strive to provide an experience that is usable, understandable, and operable for all visitors, regardless of how they access our site.

Conformance Target

We aim to conform with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

Steps We've Taken

  • Use semantic HTML structure (headings, landmarks, lists) so screen readers can navigate the page.
  • Provide text alternatives for images, including descriptive alt attributes.
  • Use sufficient color contrast in text and interactive elements.
  • Support keyboard navigation throughout the site — links, buttons, and form fields can be reached and operated without a mouse.
  • Label form fields, buttons, and interactive controls so assistive technologies can describe them.
  • Avoid auto-playing media or rapidly flashing content that could trigger seizures.
  • Design pages to be responsive so they remain usable when zoomed or viewed on small screens.

Known Limitations

We are continually working to improve accessibility. Some content on our site may not yet fully meet our accessibility goals. If you encounter content that is difficult to use or inaccessible, please let us know so we can address it.

Third-Party Content

Some content on our site is provided by third parties (such as embedded maps, social-media widgets, or video players). We do not control the accessibility of third-party content, but we will work to provide alternatives or remove inaccessible content when possible.
