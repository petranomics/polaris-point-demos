# Beacon — Full Capability Map

## How to Read This

Each capability is tagged:
- **[NOW]** — Can build today with current stack (Ollama/Claude + existing APIs)
- **[NEAR]** — Needs 1-2 new integrations but architecturally straightforward
- **[BUILD]** — Requires significant new infrastructure but fully feasible
- **[PARTNER]** — Would need a third-party service or human-in-the-loop

## STANDALONE OPPORTUNITY

If Beacon becomes its own product (not tied to Polaris Point websites):

**Name:** Beacon (standalone brand)
**Positioning:** "AI marketing team for small businesses"
**Why it works independently:**
- Most of the [NOW] capabilities don't require a website at all
- A business with a Wix/Squarespace/WordPress site still needs content, reviews, and strategy
- The AI workspace is the core product — the website is just one distribution channel

**Distribution:**
- Direct sales to businesses (same outreach pipeline you already built)
- Partner with other web agencies (they build sites, you provide Beacon)
- Marketplace/app store listings (Shopify App Store, HubSpot Marketplace)
- White-label to other agencies

**Key differentiator from competitors (Jasper, Copy.ai, etc.):**
- Beacon KNOWS their specific business (seeded with their docs, reviews, competitors)
- Not a generic writing tool — it's a dedicated marketing employee that learns their brand
- Includes monitoring + intelligence, not just content generation
- Local-first — built for Main Street, not enterprise
