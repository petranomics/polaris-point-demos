# Commission Term Sheet

**Polaris Point — Sales Representative Compensation Agreement**

This document outlines how sales commissions are calculated and paid. Read it fully before signing.

---

## 1. Commission tiers

Commission is paid on **Recognized Sale Revenue** (defined below) and steps up as you accumulate closed sales within a calendar year.

| Sales count (in calendar year) | Commission rate |
|---|---|
| Sale 1 through 5 | **15%** |
| Sale 6 through 10 | **20%** |
| Sale 11 and beyond | **25%** |

The rate applies to each sale individually based on which tier it falls into. Earlier sales are not retroactively re-rated when you cross a tier boundary.

### Worked example

A rep closes 12 sales in a year, each at $2,000 of Recognized Sale Revenue:

- Sales 1–5: 5 × $2,000 × 15% = **$1,500**
- Sales 6–10: 5 × $2,000 × 20% = **$2,000**
- Sales 11–12: 2 × $2,000 × 25% = **$1,000**
- **Total commission: $4,500**

---

## 2. What counts as a sale

A "sale" is counted when **all three** of the following are true:

1. A signed service agreement exists between Polaris Point and the client
2. The deposit (or first invoice for monthly-only deals) has cleared the bank
3. The handoff document is complete and approved by fulfillment

Pipeline deals, verbal commitments, and unpaid signed agreements do **not** count toward the sales tally.

---

## 3. Recognized Sale Revenue

Commission is paid on Recognized Sale Revenue per deal:

| Revenue type | Counts toward commission? |
|---|---|
| One-time setup fees (websites, ad setup, branding, etc.) | **Yes — full amount** |
| First 12 months of recurring revenue (Beacon, hosting, retainers, ads management) | **Yes — full amount** |
| Recurring revenue beyond 12 months | **No** (renewals are not commissioned) |
| Ad spend pass-through | **No** (we're paid management fees, not on spend) |
| Add-ons sold post-launch by the same rep within 30 days of launch | **Yes — counts as the same sale, no new tier credit** |
| Add-ons sold by the same rep more than 30 days post-launch | **Yes — counts as a new sale at current tier** |
| Referrals from existing clients credited to the rep who closed the original | **Yes — counts as a new sale at current tier** |

### Example calculation

Client signs the **Momentum bundle**: $1,299 setup + $678/mo recurring (Growth website + Beacon Beam on 6-mo commit + Local SEO Retainer). See `services-and-pricing.md` for current bundle composition.

- Recognized Sale Revenue = $1,299 + ($678 × 12) = **$9,435**
- If this is sale #3 of the year (15% tier): commission = **$1,415.25**
- If this is sale #8 (20% tier): commission = **$1,887.00**
- If this is sale #12 (25% tier): commission = **$2,358.75**

Note: this example was updated 2026-05-17 when the bundle was repriced from $2,499+$374/mo to $1,299+$678/mo (smaller setup, larger recurring → higher RSR per deal).

---

## 4. Payment schedule

Commission is **earned** when a sale is counted (per Section 2) and **paid** as follows:

- **Setup-fee portion:** paid in the next pay cycle after the deposit clears
- **Recurring portion:** paid monthly as each month's recurring invoice clears, for the first 12 months of the client's contract

**Pay cycle:** the 15th of each month, covering all earned commission from the prior calendar month.

---

## 5. Clawback

Commission is reversed if the client cancels within **60 days of launch**, regardless of reason. Commission already paid will be deducted from the rep's next pay cycle (or invoiced if the rep has left).

After 60 days, paid commission is final. If the client churns later, future recurring commission stops but past payments are not clawed back.

---

## 6. Discounts and pricing exceptions

- Reps may discount up to **10% off list** without approval
- Discounts of 10–20% require written approval from Pete before quoting
- Discounts above 20% reduce the rep's commission rate by 5 percentage points on that deal (e.g., a 15%-tier sale becomes 10%)

This is to prevent margin erosion. Use bundles before discounts.

---

## 7. Tier reset

Sales tally **resets on January 1 each year.** Every rep starts the new year at the 15% tier and works back up.

Existing clients carried into the new year continue to pay recurring commission to the rep who originally closed them, for the remaining portion of the 12-month commission window.

---

## 8. Splits and team deals

If two reps work the same deal, commission is split based on contribution:

- Sourced + closed: 100%
- Sourced only (handed off): 30% / 70% to the closer
- Closed only (warm lead from another rep): 70% / 30% to the sourcer

Splits must be agreed **in writing** before the deal closes. Disputes are resolved by Pete and his decision is final.

---

## 9. House accounts

The following are **not** commissionable:

- Inbound leads from polarispoint.io that contact us directly without rep involvement
- Referrals from Pete's personal network unless explicitly assigned to a rep
- Sales to family or close personal connections of any current employee or contractor

---

## 10. Termination

If the rep leaves Polaris Point (voluntarily or otherwise):

- Earned commission already paid: kept
- Commission earned but unpaid at termination: paid out on next regular pay cycle
- Future recurring commission on existing clients: **forfeited** at termination

Reps cannot solicit Polaris Point clients for 12 months after departure. This applies whether or not the rep starts a competing business.

---

## 11. What this is and isn't

- This is an **independent contractor / commission-only** arrangement unless an offer letter explicitly states otherwise.
- This is not an employment contract. Either party may end the relationship at any time with 14 days' notice.
- This document supersedes any prior verbal or written commission discussion.
