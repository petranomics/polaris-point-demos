It costs less than a freelancer and does more than an employee. $129-499/month (or $89-359 on a 6-month commit) vs. $500-5,000/month. And it never calls in sick, never has a bad day, and never forgets a deadline.

Technical

Technical Architecture (For Internal Use / Technical Buyers)

The Stack

AI Models: We use a two-tier model system. Standard tier runs on Mistral (open-source, hosted on our own servers — no data leaves our infrastructure). Premium tier uses Claude by Anthropic (best-in-class for business writing, reasoning, and strategy).

Context Engine: Every piece of business data (website content, reviews, services, brand voice, competitor intel) is stored in a structured database and loaded into the AI's context window before every generation. This is what makes it write as YOUR business, not a generic business.

Task Scheduler: Automated cron jobs trigger content generation on schedule — weekly social posts (Monday 8am), monthly newsletters (1st of month), daily review checks. No manual intervention needed.

Monitoring System: Daily automated scraping of competitor websites and review platforms. Changes are summarized by AI and flagged if significant (new services, price changes, negative review spikes).

Token Budget: Each tier has a monthly token allowance (think of it like minutes on a phone plan). This prevents runaway costs and ensures fair usage. The budget resets monthly.

Security & Privacy

Data isolation: Each client's data is stored in separate database rows with subscription-level access controls. One client cannot see another's data.

No training on your data: Your business data is used to generate content FOR you. It is never used to train AI models or shared with other clients.

Self-hosted inference: Standard tier AI runs on our own private servers (Hostinger VPS, US-based). Your data does not flow through OpenAI, Google, or any third-party AI provider unless you're on the premium Claude tier (which uses Anthropic's API with enterprise-grade data handling).

Encrypted storage: All data stored in Neon PostgreSQL with encryption at rest. API communications are TLS-encrypted.

How It Compares
Comparison

Beacon vs. Everything Else

vs. Doing Nothing ($0/mo)

Cost of inaction: Every month without marketing is a month your competitors are building their online presence while you're invisible. 76% of nearby searches lead to a same-day visit. If you're not showing up, someone else is getting that call.

vs. ChatGPT/Free AI ($20/mo)

ChatGPT: Doesn't know your business. No scheduling. No monitoring. No competitor tracking. You have to prompt it every time, and you have to remember to do it. How many times have you opened ChatGPT with good intentions and then gotten pulled away?

Beacon: Pre-loaded with your business context. Runs on schedule. Monitors competitors. Tracks reviews. Works even when you forget about it.

vs. Freelance Marketer ($500-1,500/mo)

Freelancer: Managing 10-15 clients. Uses templates. 4-8 hours/month on your account. Takes weeks to learn your business. May churn at any time.

Beacon: 100% dedicated to your business. Instant recall of everything about you. Produces more content, faster, for less. Doesn't quit.

vs. Marketing Agency ($2,000-5,000/mo)

Agency: Great for big campaigns and strategy. But 80% of what they charge goes to overhead, account management, and content production. The actual "writing social posts" part is often done by a junior employee or... AI tools they mark up 10x.

Beacon: Gives you the content production and intelligence layer at a fraction of the cost. Pair it with a strategic consultant for quarterly reviews and you have agency-level output at 1/10th the price.

vs. Hiring a Marketing Employee ($3,500-5,000/mo)

Employee: Great when you can afford it. But there's recruiting, onboarding, management, benefits, PTO, and the risk they leave after 6 months. For a small business doing $300K-1M in revenue, this is rarely justifiable.

Beacon: No recruiting, no onboarding, no PTO. Starts producing on day 1. Costs less than their first week's salary — every month.

vs. Jasper / Copy.ai / Other AI Tools ($50-200/mo)

Generic AI tools: They're writing tools — you give them a prompt, they give you copy. But they don't know your business, don't run on schedule, don't monitor competitors, don't track reviews, and don't improve over time with your data.

Beacon: It's not a tool you use. It's a system that works for you. The AI writing is one piece — the context engine, scheduler, monitor, and workspace make it a complete marketing operation.
