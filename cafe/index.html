Our Story

Born on South Congress

Sidecar Coffee Co. started in 2021 with a simple idea: serve exceptional coffee in a space that feels like home. We source every bean from Texas roasters, bake with local partners, and believe that a great cup of coffee can make someone's entire day better.

Locally Roasted Beans
