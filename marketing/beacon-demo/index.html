Good question -- this is one of the most important conversations you'll have with a seller. Here's how I'd approach it, based on the current 78704 market:

Lead with respect for their number:

"I understand why you're thinking $700K -- you've put a lot into this home, and Bouldin Creek is a strong market. Let me show you exactly what the data says so we can price it to get you the best possible result."

  • 1620 S 2nd St -- similar size/age, sold for $618K (March 2026, 9 DOM)

"At $700K, we'd be priced 12% above every comparable sale in the neighborhood.
