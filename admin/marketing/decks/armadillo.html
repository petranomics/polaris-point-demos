Why agencies trust the data

Multi-account, with hard guarantees

Three handles per platform per login. Each one scoped end-to-end — switching accounts swaps the dashboard, swaps the kit, swaps everything. No bleed, ever.

Per-account snapshot scoping

Every analytics snapshot is keyed to platform-username. The kit reads only the active account's data. Cleanup runs on every mount to delete legacy unscoped keys.

Repost / non-authored filter

TikTok reposts, LinkedIn reshares, Twitter retweets — all dropped at the scrape layer. The dashboard reflects what the creator wrote, not what they amplified. Brands check; the numbers hold.
